Information We Collect

Files You Upload

When you use {{ app_name }}, we temporarily process the files you upload for conversion. These files are:

  • Stored temporarily in secure servers during processing
  • Automatically deleted within 24 hours of upload
  • Never permanently stored or backed up
  • Processed in isolated, secure environments

Technical Information

We may collect limited technical information to improve our service:

  • IP address (for security and abuse prevention)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system information
  • File format and size statistics (anonymized)
  • Service usage patterns (anonymized)

Information We Don't Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Content of your files or documents
  • File names or metadata beyond technical requirements
  • Location data or device identifiers

File Handling & Processing

Upload Process

When you upload files to {{ app_name }}:

  • Files are transmitted using HTTPS encryption
  • Each file receives a unique, random identifier
  • Files are stored in temporary, secure storage
  • No human personnel access your files during processing

Conversion Process

During file conversion:

  • Automated systems handle all processing
  • Files are processed in isolated, secure containers
  • No file content is logged or monitored
  • Conversion happens on secure, dedicated servers

File Access

Access to your files is strictly limited:

  • Only you can access your converted files via unique download links
  • Download links expire within 24 hours
  • No {{ app_name }} staff can access your files
  • Files are automatically purged from all systems after 24 hours

How We Use Your Data

We use the limited data we collect only for:

  • Service Provision: Converting your files to the requested format
  • Security: Preventing abuse and protecting our infrastructure
  • Technical Improvement: Analyzing anonymous usage patterns to improve performance
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal obligations and responding to lawful requests

We do NOT use your data for:

  • Marketing or advertising purposes
  • Building user profiles or tracking across sites
  • Selling or renting to third parties
  • Any purpose unrelated to file conversion

Data Sharing & Third Parties

No Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your files or personal information with third parties for commercial purposes.

Service Providers

We may work with trusted service providers who assist in operating our service:

  • Cloud hosting providers (with strict data processing agreements)
  • Security monitoring services
  • Technical infrastructure providers

These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and cannot use it for their own purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as:

  • In response to valid legal process (subpoenas, court orders)
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users
  • In connection with legal investigations

Security Measures

Data Protection

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Servers: Files are processed on secure, monitored servers
  • Access Controls: Strict access limitations and authentication
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Isolation: Each conversion runs in isolated environments

Infrastructure Security

Our technical security includes:

  • Firewall protection and intrusion detection
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Secure backup and disaster recovery procedures
  • 24/7 security monitoring and incident response
  • Industry-standard security certifications

Data Retention

File Retention

Your uploaded and converted files are:

  • Automatically deleted from all systems within 24 hours
  • Not backed up or archived anywhere
  • Completely purged from temporary storage
  • Irrecoverable after deletion

Technical Data

Anonymous technical data may be retained for:

  • Service improvement and optimization
  • Security monitoring and abuse prevention
  • Legal compliance requirements

This data is anonymized and cannot be linked back to specific users or files.

Cookies & Analytics

Essential Cookies

We use minimal, essential cookies for:

  • Maintaining your session during file conversion
  • Remembering your preferences (like theme settings)
  • Ensuring security and preventing abuse

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand:

  • General usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Performance metrics
  • Error rates and technical issues

All analytics data is anonymized and aggregated.

No Tracking

We do not use:

  • Third-party advertising cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Cross-site tracking technologies
  • User profiling or behavioral targeting

Your Privacy Rights

Data Control

You have the right to:

  • Delete: Your files are automatically deleted within 24 hours
  • Access: Request information about any data we may have
  • Correct: Update any inaccurate information
  • Object: Opt out of any non-essential data processing

GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you're in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to be forgotten
  • Right to lodge complaints with supervisory authorities

CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights
